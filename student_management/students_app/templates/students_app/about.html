About the Student Management Portal

Welcome to the Student Management Portal, a simple and efficient way to manage student information. This platform is designed to help administrators and staff members keep track of important student details such as name, age, email, and address.

Our portal allows you to:

  • Add New Students - Enter all the necessary information for a new student.
  • Update Student Information - Edit or modify existing student data.
  • List All Students - View a comprehensive list of all registered students.
  • Delete Students - Remove student information that is no longer needed.

The Student Management Portal was created to provide an easy-to-use interface for managing and organizing student data. It is designed to be user-friendly and scalable, making it ideal for small to medium-sized educational institutions.
